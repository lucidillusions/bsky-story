>“Rage is inefficient, it requires staff to scrape incompetent remnants off the floor and file reports with the Department of Extraneous Corporeal Removal. I do not rage, instead, I merely tap my quill on the obsidian conference table. It is enough”

<hr />

My first acceptance with MetaStellar, although this story is a reprint.
Go give [Kiss To Forget](https://www.metastellar.com/fiction/flash-fiction/kiss-to-forget/), and enjoy this short cozy fok-horror.


I initially wrote this story for Apex Magazine's Monthly Flash Fiction for the theme "Cat Got Your Tongue" and after rejection, it was housed at my [Patreon (for members only section)](https://patreon.lucidillusions.in/). Recently I've found out that there are some markets that accept reprints, and I've started sending some of my old stories that I didn't shop around enough back then to these magazines.
